Project Vision - 
Last In First Out Looper

Conceptual Framework - 
Geological stratta form as loops stack.
As loop layers are burried they become decreasingly audible.
Archeological excivation and erosion of layers return them to the audible zone, but modified by the pressures they experienced while burried. 
Pressure builds as layers are burried deeper.
Environmental factors effect layers until they are burried. - erosion, weather, etc.
Extreme environmental factors are allowed to effect layers below within specific constraints.

Effects and Modulation-

Granular synthesis engine - capable of creating fine grains or chunking and combining layers. Fine blowing grains in sand, pressure squishing the edges of layers together in muddy environments, Cracking layers shifting and recombining diring seismic activity.

White/Pink/Brown noise generator - running in concert with audio recordings. eg. undulating waves in the top layer of Sea, whipping winds in mountain, deep shifting pressures.

Bi-quad Filter / Chebychev Filter - cutoff and resonance - High pass, low pass, band pass, notch, peak
